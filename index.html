How It Works


Create Ride Offer

A driver creates a ride offer on the application for a particular destination.

Request Ride

A passenger request for a available ride offers to a destination, and waits for the driver to either accept or reject the offer.

Take Ride

After successful request has been accepted. Meet driver at pickup location and enjoy your ride.

Your biggest concern is ours as well. We have ensured that people on this platform are verified and our rating system puts your mind at ease.

    Passenger

  1. Enter your trip information and click on the "search" button OR join any of the already displayed rides.

  2. Available rides that match your route will be displayed and you can join any of the rides.

  3. To ensure the platform is safe, you need to sign up and verify your account using your National Identity as well as verify your phone number.

  4. The first ride joined on the platform is 50 percent off (Paid for by the company). This means if you use our platform for your next trip, your ride will be half the original price for tat trip.

  5. You will get notified once the ride owner has confirmed your ride request.

    Driver

  1. Post a trip by entering your origin and destination.

  2. Recieve bookings when passenger pay online in advance

  3. Meet and drive. Get paid 48 hours after trip has ended and confirmed by passenger. Only exception to this will be if issues arise

  4. To ensure the platform is safe, you need to sign up and verify your account using your National Identity as well as verify your phone number.

  5. Ride Link Up admin will vet your ride before it is published on the platform.

  6. Once it is published, you will start receiving ride request notifications from users for you to accept.
